# Hi, I’m Lynn

I’m a multidisciplinary AI engineer with roots in computational neuroscience, machine learning, and software development. I care deeply about ideas, especially when they push boundaries or help us understand something more clearly or differently.

With a PhD in AI and a background that blends science with creativity, I love working on projects that explore the connection between brains and machines. I'm especially drawn to tools and systems that make a real impact, whether by improving lives, enabling expression, or challenging assumptions.

Creativity matters to me because it's how we bridge disciplines, express our values, and imagine better futures. It helps me stay curious, open, and playful, even when building serious or technical things.

I enjoy collaborating on meaningful AI projects, particularly the kind that feel ambitious, thoughtful, and grounded in a desire to make the world better.
